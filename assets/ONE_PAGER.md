## The Problem

Your sales team is drowning in leads. They spend hours:
- Reading form submissions
- Researching companies
- Writing personalized emails
- Deciding who's worth their time

Meanwhile, hot leads go cold.

---

## The Solution

**Website Lead Autopilot** does the work in seconds:

1. **Form comes in** → Webhook triggers instantly
2. **Lead qualified** → AI scores 0-100 based on fit, intent, timing
3. **Company researched** → Industry, size, recent news extracted
4. **Email drafted** → Personalized message referencing their specific needs
5. **You review (optional)** → Approve in queue or auto-send trusted leads
6. **Lead logged** → Full audit trail in your CRM
